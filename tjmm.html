Tu Jhoothi Main Makkaar abbreviated as TJMM, is a 2023 IndianHindi-language romantic comedy film directed by Luv Ranjan and written by Rahul Mody and Ranjan. Produced by Luv Films and T-Series Films, it stars Ranbir Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor, following a hiatus of three years,alongside Dimple Kapadia and Anubhav Singh Bassi. Boney Kapoor makes his first screen appearance as an actor, while Monica Chaudhary, Inayat Verma, Hasleen Kaur, Jatinder Kaur, Amber Rana, Rajesh Jais andAyesha Raza Mishra play supporting roles.
