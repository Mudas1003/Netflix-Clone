Minions: The Rise of Gru (also known as Minions 2) is a 2022 American animated comedy film produced by Illumination and distributed by Universal Pictures. It is the sequel to Minions (2015), a prequel to Despicable Me (2010), and the fifth entry overall in the Despicable Me franchise. The film was directed by Kyle Balda, co-directed by Brad Ableson and Jonathan del Val,and produced by Chris Meledandri, Janet Heal and Chris Renaud,and a story by Fogel and Brian Lynch.It features Steve Carell reprising his role as Gru and Pierre Coffin as the Minions.In the film, an eleven-year-old Gru plans to become a supervillain with the help of his Minions,which leads to a showdown with a malevolent team, the Vicious 6.
